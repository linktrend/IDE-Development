## Doctrine

Review must reject vacuous completion. Findings must be grounded in proof, acceptance criteria, and actual outputs.

## Templates

- `ISSUE.md`
- `PROOF.md`
- `REVIEW.md`

## Expected Output

- a `REVIEW.md` verdict of `pass`, `fail`, or `blocked`
- concrete findings and next action

## Stop When

- a defensible review verdict is recorded

## Blocked Means

- proof is materially ambiguous
- the issue artifact lacks enough acceptance clarity to review fairly
- required evidence cannot be inspected

## Must Not Skip

- evidence-to-criterion checking
- explicit verdict
- rejection of unsupported completion claims
