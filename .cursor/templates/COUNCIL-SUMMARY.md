## Council Trigger Policy

Council-style review is appropriate for:

- major architecture decisions
- security, privacy, legal, or compliance risk
- release readiness disputes
- scope or venture-economics disputes
- conflicting review outcomes
- waivers of blocking gates
- module or program completion disputes
- high-impact quality or integration blockers

Ordinary issue review should use `REVIEW.md`, not this artifact.
